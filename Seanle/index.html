How to Play Daily Seanle

1. Collect letters hidden in the maze to spell SEAN.
2. Avoid traps, which send you back to the start.
3. Collect hourglasses to gain extra time.
4. Look out for miscolored walls that you can sneak through.
5. Beware of Sean, who appears once you collect the third letter.
